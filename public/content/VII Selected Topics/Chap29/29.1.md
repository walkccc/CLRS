## 29.1-4

> Convert the following linear program into standard form:
>
> <div>
$$\begin{array}{lccccccc}
> \text{minimize}   & 2x_1 & + & 7x_2 & + & x_3 &     & \\
> \text{subject to} &      &   &      &   & \\
>                   &  x_1 &   &      & - & x_3 & =   &  7 \\
>                   & 3x_1 & + &  x_2 &   &     & \ge & 24 \\
>                   &      &   &  x_2 &   &     & \ge &  0 \\
>                   &      &   &      &   & x_3 & \le &  0. \\
> \end{array}
$$
</div>

## 29.1-5

>
> <div>
$$\begin{array}{lccccccc}

>                   &  x_1 & + &  x_2 & - &  x_3 & \le & 7 \\
>                   & 3x_1 & - &  x_2 &   &      & \ge & 8 \\
>                   & -x_1 & + & 2x_2 & + & 2x_3 & \ge & 0 \\
