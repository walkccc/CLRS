## 31.5-1

> Find all solutions to the equations $x \equiv 4 (\mod 5)$ and $x \equiv 5 (\mod 11)$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
     m_1 & = 11, m_2 = 5. \\\\
m_1^{-1} & = 1, m_2^{-1} = 9. \\\\
     c_1 & = 11, c_2 = 45. \\\\
       a & = (c_1 \cdot a_1 + c_2 \cdot a_2) \mod (n_1 \cdot n_2) \\\\
         & = (11 \cdot 4 + 45 \cdot 5) \mod 55 = 49.
\end{aligned}
$$
